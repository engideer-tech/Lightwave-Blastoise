Shading Normals

Normal maps are crucial for realism of the scene. We believe these our very important for certain details like the trunk of a tree. Since our scene included such textures, shading normals were used. While it is used everywhere within the scene, its significance is very subtle. This can be noticed the tiny details of textured objects, like the previosuly mentioned trunk of the tree.
