Area Lights

This feature is crucial to showcase the space shuttle. The space shuttle, as displayed in several games and movies, always comprises of atleast one light which occupies a considerable area. This light provides a sense of liveness to a scene, as an area lights is predominantly visible and always captures our attention when it flickers. It opens up scope for imagination and thus, has the potential to capture the viewer in the space shuttle. It is clearly visible in our scene near the exit of the spaceship.
